About TECHNOVILLA

TECHNOVILLA is an Egyptian city on the shore of the red sea with eye-relaxing landscapes. The city has been developed since 2022 and will have its final touches during 2025. The city allows you to live a comfortable life with all facilities and entertainment needed even beyond your imagination. TECHNOVILLA offers a smart life with digital management systems for transportation, education, etc.
